AGV Fleet Optimization & WMS Integration

VisionNav Robotics  ·  Project Engineer II  ·  2024-2025

End-to-end deployment of autonomous forklift AGVs at enterprise accounts, from interpreting customer CAD drawings to optimizing routes to integrating the fleet with each customer's warehouse management system.

Situation

VisionNav deploys autonomous forklift AGVs inside customer warehouses. Each deployment is unique, different floor plans, different inventory flows, different warehouse management systems. Without careful route design and integration, AGVs create traffic conflicts, waste battery, and fail to sync with the customer's existing operations. The gap between "delivered hardware" and "running fleet" is entirely the deployment engineer's problem to solve.

Task

Own end-to-end AGV deployment for enterprise accounts, from interpreting customer CAD drawings to optimizing routes to integrating the AGV system with the customer's WMS, and be accountable for client satisfaction at go-live.

What I Did
  1. 01
    Interpreted customer environments. Read and analyzed customer CAD drawings to understand floor layouts, traffic patterns, rack configurations, pedestrian zones, and charging station placement, before any hardware was on site.
  2. 02
    Designed AGV routes. Defined vehicle paths optimizing for travel distance, turn geometry, throughput, battery efficiency, and pedestrian safety. Ran conflict analysis to prevent deadlocks between forklifts at intersections and bottlenecks.
  3. 03
    Built the WMS integration. Used SQL and REST APIs to connect the AGV dispatch system to the customer's warehouse management system, enabling seamless order-driven dispatching, AGVs receive tasks directly from the WMS without manual intervention.
  4. 04
    Managed deployment and onboarding. Acted as the primary technical interface between VisionNav engineering and the customer, running commissioning, operator training, and post-go-live support until the fleet was stable and the team was self-sufficient.
Result
50+ AGVs Deployed
10+ Enterprise Accounts
98% Client Satisfaction

Each deployment went live on schedule. The 98% satisfaction rate across 10+ accounts reflects not just functional deployments, but fleets that fit seamlessly into existing customer operations from day one.
